Registration for InWIC 2017

Registration is now open! You can download and complete the general registration form or the high school registration form, and return via email to inwic@indiana.edu.

Registration fees cover meals, snacks, program materials, and access to all program activities, including the career fair. Hotel reservations at the reduced conference rate are available until September 26, 2017. You may contact the hotel directly regarding space availability and room rates.
